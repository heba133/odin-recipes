shawerma

description

shawerma is from syria i like it a lot because it is very delicious

ingredients

  • 2lps chicken thighs
  • 3tbsp plain yougurt
  • 1ts salt
  • 3ts olive oil
  • 4cloves garlic
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • spices 2ts paprika
  • 1ts cumin
  • 1ts coriander
  • 1ts black pepper
  • 1ts salt

steps

  1. Make the marinade in a large bowl, mix yougart,olive oil, garlic, lemon juice and all the spices
  2. stir well until combined
  3. Marinate the chicken, add chicken to the marinade and mix well
  4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours [overnight is best]
  5. Cook the chicken, grill until nicely charred and cooked through
  6. Slice the chicken in to thin Sawerma-style strips
  7. Assemble the shawerma
  8. Warm the pita bread
  9. Add chicken, Veggies, and sauce
  10. Wrap tightly

Tips for best results

  • Chicken thights are better than breast [more juice]
  • Add a little vinegar or pickle juice for extra tang
  • Toast the wrap in a pan after rolling for that crispy finish
