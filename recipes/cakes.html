Chocolate Cake

description

Chocolate cake in particular is a favourite pick by me and many people. The main ingredient of the chocolate in this cake is coco which helps our brain release endorphins or the feel good chemical

ingredients

  • 220g all purpose flour
  • 75g unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 400g sugar
  • 1,5 ts baking powder
  • 1,5 ts baking soda
  • 1 ts salt
  • 2 large egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 0,5 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 ts vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot coffee or hot water

steps

  1. 1. set oven to 350 F
  2. Grease and flour a cake pan
  3. 2. in a large powel, combine Flour, cocoa powder, sugar
  4. baking powder, baking soda, salt
  5. 3. add eggs, milk, oil, vanilla
  6. mix until smooth
  7. 4. slowly stir in hot coffe or water
  8. 5. bake for 30-35 minutes
  9. check with a toothpick should come out clean
  10. 6. let the cake cool for 10-15 minutes
  11. remove from pan then slice and enjoy
